Practice Area 01

Corporate Law

Our corporate law practice provides end-to-end legal support for businesses at every stage — from incorporation and governance to complex transactions and regulatory compliance.

We advise boards, executives and shareholders on the legal frameworks that shape their enterprises, ensuring that decisions are made on a foundation of sound legal counsel.

What We Offer
  • Company incorporation and registration (CAC)
  • Corporate governance advisory
  • Shareholders' agreements and joint ventures
  • Mergers, acquisitions and due diligence
  • Regulatory compliance (SEC, CAC, NIPC)
  • Board resolutions and company secretarial services
Our Process

We begin with a thorough understanding of your business objectives. From there, we map the legal requirements, identify risks, draft the necessary instruments, and guide you through regulatory filings — all with clear timelines and transparent communication.

Practice Area 02

Property & Real Estate Law

Property is often the most significant asset a client will ever acquire. We ensure that every transaction is legally sound, every title is properly verified, and every interest is protected.

Our property practice covers the full lifecycle of real estate transactions — from initial due diligence and title searches to conveyancing, perfection of title, tenancy agreements, and the resolution of land disputes.

What We Offer
  • Title verification and search at land registries
  • Deeds of assignment, conveyance and legal mortgages
  • Perfection of title (Governor's Consent, Registration)
  • Tenancy agreements and lease documentation
  • Property litigation and recovery of premises
  • Real estate due diligence for investors
Our Process

We conduct rigorous title searches, verify the chain of ownership, identify any encumbrances, and advise you on the legal implications before you commit. We then prepare and perfect all necessary documentation, ensuring your interest is legally secured.

Secure Your Property Interest

What We Handle
  • Contractual and commercial disputes
  • Tort claims and fundamental rights enforcement
  • Debt recovery and summary judgment proceedings
  • Appeals at the Court of Appeal and Supreme Court
  • Injunctions, interlocutory and final remedies
  • Alternative dispute resolution (mediation, arbitration)
Our Process

We assess the merits of your case honestly, develop a litigation strategy aligned with your objectives, prepare processes of the highest quality, and advocate forcefully on your behalf — always keeping you informed and involved in key decisions.

Practice Area 07

Debt Recovery

Outstanding debts erode business viability. We deploy a disciplined, multi-stage approach to debt recovery — from pre-action demand and negotiation, through summary judgment proceedings, to enforcement of judgments.

Our Approach
  • Pre-action demand letters and negotiation
  • Undefended list and summary judgment procedure
  • Standard civil recovery proceedings
  • Post-judgment enforcement (garnishee, charging orders, writs)
  • Bankruptcy and winding-up petitions (where appropriate)
  • Structured settlement and repayment agreements
