Notable Engagements

Representative Legal Work

The following are illustrative examples of the types of engagements we have successfully handled. Client identities have been anonymised.

Corporate Advisory
Multi-Stage Corporate Restructuring

Advised a Lagos-based conglomerate on a multi-stage corporate restructuring involving the separation of business units, re-registration of subsidiaries, and renegotiation of key commercial contracts. The engagement spanned regulatory filings, shareholder approvals, and third-party consents.

Successfully completed within agreed timeline with zero regulatory queries.
Property Litigation
High-Value Title Dispute Resolution

Represented a property investor in a complex title dispute involving multiple competing claimants to a prime Victoria Island parcel. Conducted extensive historical title research, secured critical documentary evidence, and obtained injunctive relief pending full hearing.

Matter resolved favourably through a combination of litigation strategy and negotiated settlement.
Debt Recovery
Summary Judgment for Outstanding Commercial Debt

Initiated undefended list proceedings for the recovery of a substantial commercial debt owed to a manufacturing client. Prepared and prosecuted the application for summary judgment, overcoming the defendant's attempts to raise defences for the purpose of delay.

Judgment obtained within months; enforcement proceedings initiated.
Employment Law
Wrongful Termination at the National Industrial Court

Represented a senior executive in a wrongful termination claim against a multinational employer. The matter involved complex issues of contractual entitlement, procedural fairness, and damages for loss of office.

Favourable settlement achieved prior to full trial, preserving client's professional reputation.
Commercial Transaction
Cross-Border Joint Venture Structuring

Advised a Nigerian entity on the legal structuring of a joint venture with a foreign partner, including negotiation of the joint venture agreement, intellectual property licensing, and regulatory approvals from relevant Nigerian agencies.

Transaction successfully closed with all regulatory conditions satisfied.
Family Law
Contentious Probate & Estate Administration

Acted for beneficiaries in a contentious probate matter involving competing claims to a substantial estate. Conducted genealogical research, challenged the validity of a purported will, and protected the interests of rightful beneficiaries.

Letters of Administration granted to our clients; estate distributed according to law.
