Meet Barrister M. A. Sodipo

A Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria, called to the Bar with distinction and seasoned through years of active practice across the Federation's superior and inferior courts.

Barrister Sodipo leads the Chambers with a rare combination of scholarly depth and courtroom pragmatism. His practice spans corporate advisory, property transactions, civil and commercial litigation, debt recovery, and family law — serving a diverse clientele that includes multinational corporations, government agencies, high-net-worth individuals, and families.

His reputation is built not on volume, but on the quality of outcomes and the trust of clients who return, referral after referral, for counsel they can rely on when the stakes are highest.

BL, LL.B — Called to the Nigerian Bar
Member, Nigerian Bar Association (NBA)
Active before the Supreme Court, Court of Appeal & Federal/State High Courts
